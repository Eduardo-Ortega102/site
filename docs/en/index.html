About me

Description

Software developer in constant learning, computer science student at the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria and higher technician in computer systems and network management.

I like working in teams where communication and collaboration prevail, I believe that everyone needs to be involved to obtain the best possible result. I keep the principles "Don't Make Me Think" and "Clean Code" in mind at all time when developing user interfaces and programming.
